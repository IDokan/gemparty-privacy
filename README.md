# Privacy Policy for GemParty

Last updated: July 14, 2026
Information We Collect and Use

HamsterWheel Studio built the GemParty game as a paid commercial application. This game is purchased up-front and is intended for use as is.

We value your privacy. GemParty does not collect, store, or share any direct personally identifiable information (such as your real name, email address, physical address, or phone number).

To facilitate core gameplay features, we collect the following limited information:
1. Game Progress and Analytics

The game utilizes anonymous server logins solely to record gameplay run data (e.g., high scores, level progress, and game state). This data is used strictly to facilitate game functionality.
2. Unique Game Identifiers (Firebase UserID)

To distinguish your gameplay records and progress from other players, the game generates a random, unique alphanumeric identifier (UserID) via Google Firebase.

    This ID is completely randomized and cannot be used by us to identify your real-world identity.

    It is used solely to link your device to your specific in-game progress and high scores.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. You are advised to review this page periodically for any changes.
